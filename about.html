The Story Behind AJ Vantage

AJ Vantage was born not out of a business plan, but out of a belief — that Sri Lanka deserved a luxury leather brand that could stand with the world's finest. Founded in 2024 by M. Ashfaq Jareed, the brand began as a personal obsession with quality: the texture of full-grain leather, the precision of a hand-cut edge, the permanence of a well-made thing.

What started as a vision became a movement. Every product that leaves our studio carries with it the fingerprints of our artisans, the discipline of our engineer, and the soul of a founder who refused to compromise. AJ Vantage is not just a brand — it is a declaration that excellence can emerge from anywhere.
