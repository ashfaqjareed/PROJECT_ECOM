Payment & Pre-Deposits
Understanding our 50% upfront model

To maintain our premium service and guarantee stock allocation, AJ VANTAGE operates on a strict payment policy for high-value orders.

1. The 50% Pre-Deposit

All orders require a minimum 50% upfront payment via Bank Transfer or FriMi QR. Your order is only confirmed once our accounting team verifies the uploaded screenshot against our bank statements.

2. Balance Payment

The remaining 50% balance (if applicable) must be settled prior to dispatch, unless a Cash-on-Delivery exception is explicitly authorized by an AJ VANTAGE representative.

3. Refunds & Cancellations

The 50% pre-deposit is refundable only if the order is cancelled before shipping. Once shipped, the deposit is non-refundable unless the item is defective or incorrectly sent.

Last updated: May 2026 — AJ VANTAGE Customer Care

Shipping & Delivery
Courier timelines and risk of loss

We partner with premium local and international couriers to ensure your luxury items reach you safely and discreetly.

1. Delivery Coverage

AJ VANTAGE delivers island-wide across Sri Lanka. We also offer international shipping via DHL Express for select items.

2. Delivery Timelines

  • Colombo & suburbs: 1–2 business days after dispatch
  • Outstation: 2–4 business days after dispatch
  • Remote areas: 4–7 business days after dispatch
  • International (DHL): 5–10 business days

3. Delivery Charges

A flat delivery charge of RS. 500 applies to all orders within Sri Lanka and is added to the remaining balance — not included in the 50% pre-deposit.

4. Risk of Loss

Once your order is marked as delivered by the courier and confirmed via photo proof, AJ VANTAGE's responsibility for the item ends. Any issues must be reported within 24 hours of delivery.

Last updated: May 2026 — AJ VANTAGE Logistics Team
